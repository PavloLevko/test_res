The History of Our Congregation

The Zhovtantsi Congregation of Jehovah’s Witnesses is located in western Ukraine, near the beautiful city of Lviv. Our congregation currently has 73 active publishers who joyfully participate in the preaching work and congregation activities. At present, 7 elders lovingly care for the spiritual needs of the congregation, and 6 regular pioneers faithfully devote more time to the ministry in serving Jehovah and helping others learn Bible truths. Our Kingdom Hall was built in 1993 and continues to serve as a warm and peaceful place for worship and spiritual encouragement to this day. Over the years, the congregation has remained united and spiritually strong, supporting one another and continuing to share the good news of God’s Kingdom with people in the local area.
